    • Dear Friends,
    It is my honor to welcome you to the cybersecurity foundation community, where we work together to make the internet a safer and more secure place for everyone. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to raising awareness about cybersecurity threats and promoting best practices to protect against them. As you may know, cybersecurity threats are an ever-present danger in our increasingly digital world. From personal identity theft to corporate espionage, cyber attacks have the potential to disrupt our lives in profound ways. The mission of our foundation is to help individuals, businesses, and organizations protect themselves from these threats by providing education, resources, and support. We believe that cybersecurity is a shared responsibility. By working together, we can create a safer and more secure online environment for everyone. Our foundation is committed to building strong partnerships with businesses, governments, and other organizations to develop effective cybersecurity strategies and promote best practices. One of the key ways we accomplish our mission is through education. We offer a wide range of educational resources, including webinars, online courses, and in-person training sessions. Our goal is to provide individuals and organizations with the knowledge and skills they need to protect themselves against cyber threats. We also offer a variety of resources for businesses and organizations. These include risk assessments, security audits, and consultations on cybersecurity strategy. Our team of experts can help businesses identify vulnerabilities and develop effective strategies to protect against cyber attacks. Another important aspect of our foundation's work is research. We invest in cutting-edge research to better understand cyber threats and develop new ways to protect against them. Our team of researchers works closely with leading experts in the field to identify emerging threats and develop innovative solutions. In addition to education, resources, and research, we also provide support to individuals and organizations impacted by cyber attacks. Our team of experts can help individuals and organizations recover from cyber attacks and mitigate any damage caused. Our foundation is proud to be a part of the global cybersecurity community, and we are committed to promoting collaboration and knowledge-sharing among our peers. We participate in a variety of industry groups and associations, and we work closely with government agencies and law enforcement to promote a safe and secure online environment. We are also committed to promoting diversity and inclusivity in the cybersecurity field. We believe that a diverse workforce is essential for addressing the complex challenges of cybersecurity, and we are actively working to promote diversity in our own organization and throughout the industry. In closing, I want to thank you for joining us in our mission to create a safer and more secure online world. We are grateful for your support and look forward to working with you to achieve our shared goals. If you have any questions or would like to learn more about our foundation, please don't hesitate to contact us. We would be happy to hear from you and discuss how we can work together to promote cybersecurity awareness and education. Thank you for your commitment to cybersecurity and for joining us in this important work.

    Sincerely,
    Ahmet Mithat Demirkol
